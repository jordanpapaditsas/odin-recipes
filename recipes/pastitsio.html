Pastitsio

Homemade Greek Pastitsio

Pastitsio is a Greek baked pasta dish with ground meat and béchamel sauce, with variations of the dish found in other countries of the Mediterranean Sea.

Ingredients

  • 750gr ground beef
  • 2 onions
  • 1 clove(s) of garlic
  • 1 tbsp thyme
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbps tomatoe paste
  • 100ml red wine
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 100g butter
  • 1 liter whole milk
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 100g all-purpose flour
  • 300g bucatini pasta
  • 200g feta cheese
  • 50g gruyere cheese
  • 1/2 bunch parsley

Steps

  1. Place a pan over high heat. Add 2 tablespoons of olive oil, the finely chopped onions, Sauté for 3-4 minutes, until the onions caramelize nicely.
  2. Add the garlic and continue to sauté.
  3. Add the tomato paste and sauté for at least 2 minutes. It is important to sauté the tomato paste to give it a more intense flavor.
  4. Add the ground meat. Break it up with a wooden spoon and brown for 4-5 minutes. At this point you may need to add another 2 tablespoons of olive oil.
  5. Add the wine and let the alcohol evaporate. Add the canned tomatoes.
  6. When ready, remove from heat.
  7. Heat the butter in a pot over medium heat. As soon as it melts (do not let it burn), add the flour. Beat with a hand whisk and sauté the flour for a few minutes.
  8. Add the milk, in small batches, whisking continuously so that no lumps form in the mixture.
  9. When the béchamel sauce starts to thicken, you have to be very careful not to let it burn on the bottom. Whisk continuously to avoid this.
  10. When ready, remove from heat and add salt, pepper, and the grated gruyere.
  11. To complete, add the 3 egg yolks and whisk to incorporate.
  12. Add the pasta to a pot full of salted water. Boil 1-2 minutes less than the instructions on the box. They need to be al dente because they will cook further in the oven, so do not overcook.
  13. When the pasta is ready, drain and spread in the baking pan.
  14. Add feta cheese, season to taste and set aside until needed.
  15. Preheat oven to 180* C (350* F) Fan. Bring back the baking pan containing the pasta mixture.
  16. To the ground meat, add a ladleful of the béchamel sauce. Mix until completely incorporated.
  17. The béchamel sauce will make the ground meat mixture hold together better when serving.
  18. Spread the ground meat mixture over the pasta in the baking pan.
  19. Pour the remaining béchamel sauce over the ground meat.
  20. Bake for 40 minutes or until the béchamel turns golden brown.
